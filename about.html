🎵 Can Music Inspire Interest in Other Cultures?

An interactive international research project exploring how music influences cultural curiosity.

🎯 Project Goal

This project explores whether listening to music from different countries encourages people to become interested in learning about those cultures.

❓ Research Question

Can music inspire people to discover another country's culture?

💡 Hypothesis

Music creates emotional connections that make people more curious about traditions, languages and everyday life in another culture.

🔬 Research Process

🌍 Choose a country
🎧 Listen to a popular song
📖 Learn cultural facts
📝 Complete a short survey
📊 View live global results

🌎 Countries Included

🇮🇹 Italy

Rock, opera, fashion and Mediterranean culture.

🇯🇵 Japan

Anime music, J-Pop and centuries-old traditions.

🇰🇷 South Korea

K-Pop, innovation and contemporary culture.

🇫🇷 France

French chanson, art, fashion and history.

🇪🇸 Spain

Flamenco, festivals and vibrant traditions.

🇧🇷 Brazil

Samba, Carnival and cultural diversity.

📊 What Do We Measure?

👥 Familiarity

How well participants knew the country before listening.

⭐ Song Rating

How much participants enjoyed the music.

😊 Emotions

The emotions created by the song.

❤️ Cultural Interest

Whether participants want to learn more afterwards.

💬 Reflection

The most interesting discovery made during the experience.

🌍 Why This Research Matters

Music is often the first step toward understanding another culture. Even one song can encourage people to explore a country's traditions, language, history and everyday life. This project investigates whether music can become a bridge between cultures.

❤️ Personal Motivation

As someone passionate about singing, songwriting and international education, I wanted to explore whether music could inspire curiosity about other cultures. I believe music has the power to connect people beyond language and geography, encouraging empathy, openness and global understanding.
