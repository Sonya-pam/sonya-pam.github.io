# 🎵 Can Music Inspire Interest in Other Cultures?

An interactive research project exploring how music influences curiosity about different cultures.

## 🌍 Features

- Interactive country pages
- Spotify music player
- Cultural facts
- Survey
- Live statistics
- Google Sheets integration

## 🎯 Research Goal

To explore whether listening to music from another country encourages interest in its culture.

- 🇯🇵 Japan
- 🇰🇷 South Korea
- 🇪🇸 Spain
